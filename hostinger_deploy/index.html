Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using our Shopify import assistant tool.

Is it safe to scrape Amazon product links?

Scraping details for private use is standard practice. However, our app warns you against copying copyrighted images or texts blindly. We recommend utilizing our auto-rewrite engine and setting authorized product photos.

How do I import the generated CSV sheet into Shopify?

Simply export the CSV from the app, open your Shopify Admin, click on 'Products', select 'Import', choose your file, and hit upload. The file matches Shopify's native multi-image mapping rules.

Does this app save my API keys or supplier data?

No, configurations and imported listings are stored inside your secure local app database environment, ensuring complete data sovereignty and privacy.
