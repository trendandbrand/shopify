Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

At Product2Shopify AI ("we," "our," or "us"), we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information collected and recorded by Product2Shopify AI (hosted on ecomtool.online) and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

If you use our services, we do not store your private credentials or API keys on external cloud servers. All application configurations, including temporary product imports and custom SKU settings, are stored locally within the application database container or browser cache depending on session deployment.

We may collect standard log files that register visitors when they access the landing page website. These logs contain Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. This data is used solely to analyze web traffic trends and administer site speed improvements.

2. Cookies

Like any other website, ecomtool.online uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

3. Third-Party Services

Since our application integrates frames running on cloud infrastructure, please note that those platforms may have their own privacy policies regarding connection data, session cookies, and database state management. We advise you to consult their respective policies for detailed practices.

4. Data Sovereignty

Any product files (CSV/Excel) generated by you are compiled on the server and downloaded immediately. We do not keep copies of your product spreadsheets or business catalogs on our landing web server.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
